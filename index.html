Project

Application of Data Mining Using the C4.5 Method to Predict Sales in Wingman Denim

  • Repository
  • Article
  • This research employs the C4.5 method to predict denim sales at Wingman Denim based on sales data from 2022, comprising 440 instances.
  • The study utilizes 8 crucial sales attributes, namely selvedge, fabric weight, source, cutting , size, color, price, and total sales.
  • Pre-processing encompasses attribute reduction and data cleansing to ensure dataset reliability.
  • Data transformation involves the categorization of continuous variables using LabelEncoder.
  • The C4.5 decision tree model is employed for sales prediction.
  • The evaluation results demonstrate optimal success with an accuracy rate of 100%, illustrating the potential of this model to enhance understanding and predict denim product sales at Wingman Denim.
